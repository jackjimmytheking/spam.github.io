Government Cybersecurity Alert

The site you are attempting to reach is no longer available.

This domain has been flagged and removed by the National Bureau of Consumer Protection. Evidence gathered indicates that this site was being utilized for criminal scamming and phishing activities amounting to a total of $32.8 million dollars.

Investigation Findings: Evidence indicates that all romantic or friendly relationships established on this platform were entirely fraudulent. Digital forensics revealed that these interactions were not real; they were orchestrated by organized groups operating out of Turkey. These actors utilized Generative AI to produce synthetic imagery and deceptive text messaging to mimic human connection. This was a deliberate ploy to emotionally manipulate victims and extract money through the purchase and transfer of platform coins.
